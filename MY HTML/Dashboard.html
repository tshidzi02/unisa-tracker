myModules is UNISA’s official collaborative teaching and learning environment. It is used to support online and blended courses as well as other UNISA-related groups and communities. 

Our virtual platform is built on Moodle open source software. myModules provides you access to all your current module sites, as well as project sites if you are linked to any.

Here are a few handy tips to get you started:

1. Find all your modules under the myModules menu above the dark grey banner.

2. By clicking on your initials in the top right-hand corner you can:

  • Access your profile, grades, preferences and messages is available.
