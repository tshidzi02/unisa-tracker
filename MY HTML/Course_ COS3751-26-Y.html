    Welcome to COS3751: Techniques of Artificial Intelligence

    Dear Student(s),

    This page is dedicated to supporting your learning for this module. Mastery of the content in this module will provide you with a basic understanding of some of the standard techniques of artificial intelligence, including knowledge representation and reasoning, state-space searching, and automated learning.

    Please note that this is a fully online module and all the information will be available via the myUnisa platform. You are encouraged to log into the module site on myUnisa, on a daily basis. The module website is COS3751-26-Y. Because this is a fully online module, you will need to use myUnisa to study and complete the learning activities for this module.

    If you make a regular habit of visiting your module site, you can take full advantage of these features:

    • Learning Units: For fully online modules, please visit Learning Units for study materials. Any other documents provided by your lecturer will be available in the Additional Resources tool.
    • Discussion Forum: This will allow you to talk with your fellow students about this course. The lecturers may include other Topics and Forums.
    • Assessments: The assessments (assignments) will be released one at a time. Note that the first assignment will be in the form of online MCQs, while the 2nd and 3rd will be written and might be comprised on long questions.

    Other things you might find in your module webpage:

    Your lecturer may use the Announcements tool to inform you about monthly sessions (lessons conducted via MS-Teams), special meetings, or any important course-related issues. When they put an Announcement, you may also receive an email reminding you to check the website.

    We hope that you will take full advantage of these additional features to make your studies more exciting and successful. We hope that you will enjoy this module and wish you every success in your studies!

    Contact Details:
