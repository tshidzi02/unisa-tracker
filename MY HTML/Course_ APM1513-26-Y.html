      Welcome Message

      Welcome to APM1513: APPLIED LINEAR ALGEBRA

       

       

      Purpose: To enable students to master and apply the following aspects of the numerical solution of systems of linear equations: the method of least squares; linear programming (simplex method); eigenvalues, eigenvectors, diagonalization as well as some miscellaneous applications.

      At level 1 of your studies, this module marks the start of computational methods and computer use in applied mathematics. The other undergraduate modules connected to APM1513 are COS2633 Numerical Methods I, APM2616 Computer Algebra, and APM3711 Numerical Methods II at levels 2 and 3, respectively. While APM2616 demonstrates how to solve problems symbolically, this module APM1513, COS2633, and APM3711 are all about finding numerical solutions to problems.
